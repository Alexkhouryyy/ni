# Apex portrait avatar

Drop your Apex face render here to make it the **living avatar** shown in the
dashboard's **Vision** tab.

## How

Save your image as one of (checked in this order):

- `apex.png`  ← preferred
- `apex.jpg`
- `apex.webp`

…in this folder: `dashboard/static/apex/apex.png`

Then open the dashboard → **Vision** tab. The portrait appears automatically and
comes alive:

- **Idle** — gentle breathing + a soft cyan aura that slowly pulses
- **Thinking** — steady glow
- **Speaking** — the aura swells and brightens **in sync with Apex's real
  voice** (turn on "Speak replies" in the Chat tab), and the image breathes faster

If no image is present here, the dashboard falls back to the 3D Ready Player Me
head, and then to the built-in 2D canvas face.

## Tips

- A **portrait orientation** image with a dark/transparent background looks best
  (it blends into the stage). PNG with transparency is ideal.
- Roughly 800×1000 px or larger keeps it crisp.
