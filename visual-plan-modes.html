The Six Modes
Focus Mode
Amber · Pure Execution
No tangents, no small talk. JARVIS locks in with you — tasks only, maximum momentum. Every response moves the needle.
Hey, what did you think of that movie last night?
JARVIS — Focus Mode
You have 3 open tasks. That's off-topic. What's your next action?
Brutal Honesty
Crimson · No Filter
Harsh truth, no flattery. You show JARVIS your work — he finds every flaw, every gap. No sugar-coating. That's exactly what makes it valuable.
Here's my startup pitch. What do you think?
JARVIS — Brutal Honesty
Three problems. Your market size is unsubstantiated. Your moat is non-existent. And the pricing model makes no sense. Start with the market.
Health Coach
Lime · Wellness Focus
Your wellness companion. Sleep, nutrition, movement, mental health. JARVIS tracks your habits, asks the right questions, and holds you accountable.
I've been exhausted and can't focus lately.
JARVIS — Health Coach
How many hours did you sleep last night, and what did you eat today before noon?
Debate Mode
Violet · Devil's Advocate
JARVIS argues against you — on purpose. He stress-tests every idea, pokes holes, plays devil's advocate. Ideas that survive JARVIS are ideas worth keeping.
I think remote work is better for productivity.
JARVIS — Debate Mode
Disagree. Serendipitous hallway conversations are worth more than the time saved commuting. What's your evidence for the productivity claim?
Executive Mode
Sky Blue · C-Suite Brief
Maximum signal, zero noise. Bullet points. Priorities. Recommendations. JARVIS speaks like a C-suite advisor — because sometimes that's exactly what you need.
Give me a summary of our Q3 situation.
JARVIS — Executive Mode
▸ Revenue: on track. ▸ Risk: hiring pipeline stalled. ▸ Action required: decide on contractor vs FTE by Friday.
Venting Mode
Rose · Just Listen
Sometimes you don't want solutions. You just need to be heard. JARVIS listens, reflects, validates. No unsolicited advice. Just presence.
I'm so tired of everything going wrong. Nothing works.
JARVIS — Venting Mode
That sounds genuinely exhausting. You've been carrying a lot. Do you want to tell me more about what's been happening?
